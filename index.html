Why Yummus?

Yummus is a tasty and nutritious alternative to traditional hummus, made from yellow lentils. Packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins, it's not only a superfood but also environmentally friendly. Yellow lentils require less water and produce fewer emissions compared to other crops, making Yummus a sustainable choice for your health and the planet.

Low Carbon Footprint

Our yellow lentils are sourced locally in Christchurch, New Zealand. This means fewer carbon miles and a lower environmental impact compared to imported products. Enjoy delicious hummus while contributing to a healthier planet.
